## What Malaysian business owners ask most

### How soon will I see results from digital marketing?

Google Ads and Meta campaigns typically generate qualified leads within 10–14 days. SEO is a longer game, with meaningful traffic movement usually visible between months 3 and 6. Cressoft recommends an integrated approach — paid ads for immediate leads while SEO builds your long-term organic asset.

### Do you work with SMEs, or only large companies?

The majority of Cressoft clients are Malaysian SMEs — businesses with 5 to 200 staff who need proper marketing but don't require a full in-house team. Every engagement is scoped to the actual size and stage of the business.

### Do you only operate in Kuala Lumpur?

No. Cressoft works with businesses across Malaysia — from Penang to Johor Bahru, Kota Kinabalu to Kuching — and has delivered campaigns for clients in Singapore and wider Southeast Asia. Day-to-day communication runs through WhatsApp, email, and Zoom.
